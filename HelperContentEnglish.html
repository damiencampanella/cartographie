Menu area:

The "Lines" tab is used to select the lines your are interested in

Hover the mouse over a line to hide the others

Hide lines This button makes the other lines become greyed instead of hidden

Click on a line to deselect it

This button hides all the lines of its corresponding category


The "Selection" tab is used to choose the direction and the variation for each line

Click on one line to display its different variations and the stations list of the selected variation

This button changes the direction

This button is used to deselect the line

Click on any variant to select it

Hover the mouse over one of the stations of the list to display the corresponding information

On the right to the station is displayed the possible connections

Remove all selected lines This button is used to deselect all the lines


The "Vehicles" tab contains the list of the vehicle which have a sensor

Use the search field to search among the vehicles

The search can be made by name, by stock number or by sensor ID

Hover the mouse over one of the vehicles of the list to display the corresponding information

Click on a vehicle to zoom on it

Hide the vehicles This button masks all vehicles
